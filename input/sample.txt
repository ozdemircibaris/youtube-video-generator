Hello! Today we're going to learn some common English phrases that you can use in everyday conversation.

The first phrase is "Nice to meet you." This is what you say when you are introduced to someone for the first time.

The second phrase is "How are you doing?" This is a common greeting that asks about someone's well-being.

The third phrase is "I see what you mean." This indicates that you understand someone's point of view.

The fourth phrase is "That makes sense." Use this when you agree with someone's reasoning.

The last phrase we'll learn today is "Let's keep in touch." This is what you say when you want to maintain contact with someone.

Practice these phrases in your daily conversations to improve your English fluency!
